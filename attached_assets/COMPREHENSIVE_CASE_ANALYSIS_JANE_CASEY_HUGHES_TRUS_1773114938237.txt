**Carolina Reid** - Jane's daughter, successor trustee of the trust, and held financial/healthcare power of attorney. Lives at 1010 W Milton St, Austin, Texas. Works in insurance sales with limited personal income.

**John-Michael Kuczynski** - Jane's son, trust beneficiary, academic/author. Lives in New York. Filed multiple legal actions to compel proper trust administration.

**Alexandra Kuczynski** - Jane's daughter, trust beneficiary. Lives at 471 Northwood Way, Ketchum, ID 83340. Worth several million from divorce settlement. Represented by Shulman Rogers attorneys but has taken largely passive approach to litigation.

**The Trust** - Jane Casey Hughes Revocable Trust established August 23, 1996, governed by Maryland law. Contains mandatory distribution language requiring "outright and free of trust" distributions to beneficiaries upon settlor's death. Trust assets valued at approximately $8.2 million at time of Jane's death.
